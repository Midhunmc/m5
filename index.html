Very few sedans generate as much heart beating and adrenaline as the BMW M5. Especially when it comes in a limited Special Edition with Competition Package. Cast in the visually stunning BMW Individual special paint finish: Mineral White metallic, this exceptional BMW M5 also sets the pulses racing with its elegant side.

In this Special Edition, the performance of the powerful 4.4 litre M TwinPower Turbo 8-cylinder petrol engine has been increased to a breathtaking 600 HP (441 kW).
A modified suspension with firmer spring and damper set-up ensures even more dynamic control. The specially adjusted ride height lowered by 10 millimetres provides more stability, both on the race track and in everyday use. The steering with M specific Servotronic Mode and directly configured characteristic curve ensure that every steering command is performed with maximum precision.



The BMW M5 unites two worlds more than any other car: ultimate motor sport feeling and everyday suitability. The already dynamic look is boosted once again by the BMW M Performance Parts.

EXCLUSIVE EXTERIOR HIGHLIGHTS OF THE SPECIAL EDITION:
  • M gills element with “M5 Competition” logo
  • 20” BMW M light alloy wheels double-spoke 601 M with exclusive paint in Jetblack uni, gloss-lathed
  • Twin tailpipe of the exhaust system in black chrome
  • BMW M Performance front grille in black
  • BMW M Performance door mirror caps Carbon
  • BMW M Performance rear diffuser Carbon
  • BMW M Performance rear spoiler Carbon

EXCLUSIVE INTERIOR HIGHLIGHTS OF THE SPECIAL EDITION
  • BMW M multifunctional seats in fine-grain Merino leather Black
  • BMW Individual Manufaktur: contrast seams in Opal White.
  • BMW Individual Manufaktur: headrests "M5" embroidery
  • BMW Individual Manufaktur: Floor mats in Velour with bordering in BMW Individual fine-grain Merino leather in Opal White
  • BMW M Performance interior trims Carbon with “M5 Competition - 1/200” signet

The BMW M5 Competition Edition continues its exceptionally dynamic look in both the exterior and the interior. The BMW Individual Manufaktur sets unparalleled aesthetic standards, which give it a thoroughly exclusive character
